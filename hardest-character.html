The Hardest Chinese Character

票 （piào)

我觉得 that 是 the hardest chinese character for 我 right now。
票的意思 is ticket，vote，or coupon. This chinese character is widely use on many chinese words such as 门票（mén piào - ticket for theather or cinema)，托票（tóu piào - to vote/vote)，or 钞票（chāo piào - paper money）。There are 三个radicals in this character. The First radical is 覀(yà) which means west, the second one is 示 (shì) which means spirit. 票 character has 十一 strokes。 The order of the strokes are héng, shù, héng zhé, shù, shù, héng, héng, héng, shù gōu, piě, 和 diǎn.


Other definitions for 票 （piào)：slip of paper or bamboo, person held for ransom, amateur performance of Chinese opera; measure word for groups, batches, business transactions.

My own experience: I have known the 票（piào) character for a long time. My first time I saw the character was 漂亮 （piào liang)。Even though there 是 some additional strokes (3 strokes) and 两 radical (丶- diǎn - dot 和 冫- bīng - ice), The base character 是 票。我觉得这个是 the most difficult character for me 因为 of the amount of the strokes that 我 need to write (十一 strokes which 我常常forget)，I can remember, speak and repeat the word many times, but I don't identify the exact character (which leads me to change my study methods and techniques) 和 sometimes I forgot to 学习 the radicals for this specific character。 Even though I have this difficulties with this character, 我觉得 that it's important to learn it 因为 这个 character can be use in a lot of context 因为 of the different meanings.
